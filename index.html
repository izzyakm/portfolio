Project 2: Agrow Toolbox

On 20 December 2019, I joined Agro-food productivity Nexus, under the management of Malaysia Productivity Corporation (MPC) competition. The initiative focuses on productivity development in building connectivity of the agro-food supply chain. The information toolbox target users all Malaysian to access agro-food information to increase productivity. The information toolbox made finding and access information easy and fun to use. Simple logic instruction to guide from less tech-savvy to high tech-savvy visitors. The toolbox incorporated UIUX design to capture the attention of the users.
