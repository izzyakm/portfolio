Objective

The objective of this website is to enable easy, free, fast accessibility to the Malaysian agro-food information to increase the stakeholder competitiveness and productivity of their businesses. Include information, such as historic knowledge-based/records, current data/news, and future intelligence/predictions. Agrofood information includes the field of production, distribution, consumption, sustainability and environment and acts as a toolbox.

Responsibilities

For this project, I took a role as a UI UX designer and graphic designer. My responsibilities included: Visual Design, Prototyping, Usability Testing and Develop high fidelity mock-ups.

The challenge is about taking things that are infinitely complex and making them simpler and more understandable. — Robert Greenberg
The Problem

The main problem in the agriculture sector is the lack of knowledge transfer and inadequate connectivity and communication within the chain entities. Because of this gap, there is inconsistency in the quality of standards and prices by local market leads to dissatisfaction and rejection by a massive corporation.

The Solution

Create an easy to use webpage and responsive mobile web solution to educate Malaysian about agriculture and help give a platform to grow their businesses from small farmer to bigger corporation. The access of the information should be a simple, easy and minimal effort for the user to research. Hence, visual clarity is taking into consideration to convey information effectively.

02 User Personas

Senin Bin Khamis is local farmer located in Sekinchan, Selangor. He has been in the agro-food industry for nearly 31 years. He took over his family business to help his late father. He is passionate to expand his business. However, he finds himself struggling to promote his business on a bigger scale as he is not a tech-savvy. He also frustrated he cannot compete with market leader due to lack of resources.

Lim Kee Ching is a 53 years old businessman. His deep determination in offering a high-quality fresh product to consumer lead him working as a middle man scouting for sustainable agriculture from a local farmer. Mr Lim finds it challenging to understand the work of the local farmer and to iterate them according to his compliant and work ethic. He also finds the struggle to provide continuity of fresh perishable to retailers with limited resources.

Fadilah Bt Kamarul is a final year student in UPM. For her Final Year Project, she needs to write a paper to improve gap technology of the local farmer. She needs a deep understanding of farmer work history and new technology to fill the gap. However, with a lack of knowledge transfer in the online presence, she has a difficult time understanding local farmers work. She also has a difficult time to find contact information online to interview a local farmer for her case study.

Ali Bin Aziz is 60 years old retired CTO in a local bank. He has always been passionate about growing fruits and flowers around his house. However, because of the tight schedule, that doesn’t allow him to have time to keep up with his hobby. Now that he has all the time in the world, he decided to start exploring his hobby again. He began to go online and study the ways of gardening, plant vegetable at the back of his house. During the learning process, Ali started to search for a community to be part of growing and learning from each other. He finds that finding a community to transfer knowledge and share knowledge is very difficult as they don’t have a platform to centralized the community to reach out quickly.

06 User-Testing

User testing conducted by using visualEyes to stimulates eye-tracking studies. After examining the generated Attention Map, the header gets a lot of attention. However, the CTA button is key to sales, traffic, and success. Hence, making the CTA button more comprehensive helps draw more attention without sacrificing the visual hierarchy.

07 Limitation

The challenges I faced during the project is the website contains too much information that needs to be condensed down to achieve a simpler and minimal webpage that can is enjoyable and easy to navigate by everyone. The website also needs to cater to the diversity of demographic. Hence, making the decision to design is very difficult. Apart from different group people, the website also caters for diverse age group and different languages to make the knowledge transfer easier from farmer to bigger corporation.

08 Retrospective

Looking back, I think the concept and the design can be iterate even further, and improve the UI UX process. After presenting the idea and design to the users and stakeholder, they provided feedback to have better consistency in design. Upon reflecting, I decided to refine the design and focus the redesigning of the webpage with consistency. Overall, it's a great experience presenting my design and collaborating with MPC X NEXUS to contribute ideas helping to modernise the agriculture sector.
