Web Design & Illustration

Objective

The objective of this website is to turn a potential client to a prospect by sign up for free consultation.

Problem Statement

The big and well-established company can be scary for small business owners to reach out. Profiterra wants to provide service to help small business owners or start-up by making them feel comfortable to reach out to discuss their business plan and help manage their accounting.

Responsibilities

For this project, I took a role as a UI UX designer and graphic designer. My responsibilities included: Visual Design, Prototyping, Usability Testing and Develop high fidelity mock-ups.

I worked alongside with a web developer to convert the prototype into a functional website.

Process

01 Research

Research conducted to get to know about the product and the competitors. There is not much online accounting in the market. However, there are a bunch of accounting software and cloud to be used to manage your business accounting. Profiterra offers to lift a massive amount of stress on SME shoulder for them to manage SME bookkeeping and accounting services.

Profiterra pricing is customizable to different SME needs and budget. It goes along with their vision to help small business owners to grow a profitable business. Profiterra also offers a free consultation for their prospect to get to know them and their vibe.

Logo Design

Profiterra objective is to help a small entrepreneur or start-up in Malaysia to move forward and grow a greater profitable business. The idea of the logo design is ‘’moving forward and greater than’’ shape that is recognisable by the human brain.

Triangle logo shape suggests stability and energy, which represents the foundation of profiterra.

06 Limitation

Profiterra strives to help many small business owners as much as they can from managing their bookkeeping to providing service as Chief Financial Officier. With their broad knowledge of accounting, they are determined to help 1 Million small business owners to grow a profitable business. Their determination to explain in great details making the website fills with detailed explanation to the potential clients. Next is readability in the mobile device. Their potential client might now be able to read the long text in mobile.

Based on research, the human brain has less attention span when reading an article. We tend to scan for keywords or graphic that catches our eyes. Hence, to overcome this challenge, each category of service provided attached a graphic/ illustration or an icon that reflect and conclude the text written. The mobile version is designed to ensure the responsiveness of the website in smaller devices.

07 Restrospective

A few things I have learned throughout this project is to have empathy towards the developer. The design needs to be easily implemented and develop and communicate with the developer during the design process to get their feedback.

Next is to understand the tool that is going to be used in the development. In this case, the developer is using WordPress. There are many limitations of the tool that need to take into consideration when designing the website.
